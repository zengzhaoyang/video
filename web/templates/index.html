Microsoft Multimedia Challenge




Recent advances in multimedia computing have opened many avenues for vision-based intelligent services. Microsoft Multimedia Challenge aims at demonstrating how these technological advances facilitate a wide range of multimedia analysis scenarios:
· Image Retrieval Challenge
· Fine-grained Image Recognition Challenge
· Video to Language Challenge
· Multimedia Q&A Challenge


News: Till 28th April, we have received 28 team registrations in Video to Language challenge.
